PERSON OF THE WEEK

Kenneth Auchenberg is the creator of ColdFront conference and founder of the CopenhagenJS meetup which has grown to more than 1,000 members over time. Passionate about the next generation of developer tooling, he worked as a Program Manager at Microsoft until recently where he was on a mission to get browsers to talk to text editors and to enable everyone to build the developer tools they need for the web. Kenneth serves on multiple advisory boards and is a Global Shaper for World Economic Forum. You can find him on Twitter as @auchenberg. Thank you for helping the web evolve, dear Kenneth!
